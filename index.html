Bio

I am an undergraduate student in School of Computing and Data Science at the University of Hong Kong (HKU). I am also fortunate to be working at HKUNLP, advised by Prof. Lingpeng Kong and Dr. Xiachong Feng. Previously, I have been an exchange student at the University of California, Berkeley in both 2023 Summer and 2024 Fall. Currently, I am also a research intern at the Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory, advised by Dr. Lijun Wu.

My current research interest includes (Multimodal) Large Language Models and its possible real-world applications.
